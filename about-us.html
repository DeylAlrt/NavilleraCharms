ABOUT us

Welcome to

Navillera Charm Studio

Where every piece is a piece of you

Navillera is all about self-expression and storytelling through charms and bracelets. Each piece is designed to reflect your personality, your memories, and your style, letting you create a bracelet that is truly yours. Our accessories are student-friendly, meaningful, and perfect for gifting, allowing everyone to wear a piece of their story.

HerStory

Founded in December 2024 by an 18-year-old university student with a dream and a vision. While working full-time and saving every penny, she turned her passion for jewelry and personal expression into a brand that empowers others to celebrate themselves.

Add charm to your life

We believe in supporting and inspiring others, especially students—giving them the opportunity to add charm to their life. Our community is built around students, creatives, and young entrepreneurs, whether through school projects, pop-ups, or campus events. We proudly allow students to use our business as part of their academic journey and are always open for partnerships and events. Navillera is also opening part-time opportunities soon, welcoming more people to grow with us.
